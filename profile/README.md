# ColorStack at Florida International University

## Building Community in Computer Science

ColorStack at FIU is a student-led community dedicated to supporting students in computing at Florida International University.

Our chapter connects students with mentorship, technical resources, and professional development opportunities while fostering an inclusive and collaborative environment within the computing community.

---

## Mission

The mission of ColorStack at FIU is to increase the number of students who graduate with computer science and engineering degrees and go on to successful careers in technology.

We support students through:

- Technical workshops
- Career preparation
- Peer mentorship
- Industry networking
- Collaborative projects
- Hackathons and innovation challenges

---

## What We Do

ColorStack at FIU organizes and supports initiatives such as:

- Technical skill-building workshops
- Resume and interview preparation
- Industry speaker events
- Mentorship opportunities
- Hackathons and coding events
- Open-source collaboration

Students from all computing disciplines are encouraged to participate.

---

## Community Impact

Through collaboration with university programs, student organizations, and industry partners, ColorStack at FIU works to strengthen representation in the technology field while helping students build the technical and professional skills needed for successful careers.
